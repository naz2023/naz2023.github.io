Introduction

Welcome to my personal website for culinary adventures.

I believe that I can reflect my passion for discovering new tastes and new places through this website. I have visited many countries and personally I could easily say that the most interesting cultural wealth is based on experiencing culinary adventures with others. In this regard, I have started to build this website to collect my personal experiences and offer inspiration for others. It is possible to define here as a tasty travel journal. I started with Turkey-my hometown, France-my favorite kitchen, Italy-land of pizza and Greece-neighbor's tasty kitchen. Let's discover and taste.
